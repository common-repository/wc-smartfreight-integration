This plugins integrates with SmartFreight online service.

- SmartFreight consolidates shipping options from multiple suppliers and provides specific delivery options to your online customers on your WooCommerce e-shop.

- Available couriers will be displayed as separate options in the checkout of your WooCommerce website along with correct pricing.

- When a SmartFreight shipping option is selected for any specific order, a consignment is automatically created in SmartFreight and you will be able to see the corresponding delivery in your SmartFreight control panel.


*** SETUP ***
To set up SmartFreight for your WooCommerce e-shop, simply install and activate the plugin.
Then, go to WooCommerce -> Settings in your admin menu.
Click into the SHIPPING tab.
Click into the SmartFreight tab.
Enter all required details and save changes.
Test the integration by going to the front-end of your website, add a product to the cart and verify that shipping options appear correctly in your checkout.
